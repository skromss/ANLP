# Music Theory Notes

## 1. Basics
- **Pitch**: The highness or lowness of a sound.
- **Rhythm**: The placement of sounds in time.
- **Tempo**: The speed of the beat (BPM).

## 2. Scales
- **Major Scale**: W-W-H-W-W-W-H (W=Whole step, H=Half step). Happy sound.
- **Minor Scale**: W-H-W-W-H-W-W. Sad or melancholic sound.
- **Pentatonic Scale**: A 5-note scale, very popular in rock, blues, and pop.

## 3. Chords
- **Triad**: A chord with 3 notes (Root, 3rd, 5th).
- **Major Chord**: Root + Major 3rd + Perfect 5th.
- **Minor Chord**: Root + Minor 3rd + Perfect 5th.
- **Seventh Chord**: Triad + a 7th interval.

## 4. Circle of Fifths
A visual representation of the relationships among the 12 tones of the chromatic scale, key signatures, and related chords.
Clockwise moves by Perfect 5ths (C -> G -> D...).

## 5. Intervals
- **Perfect Unison**: 0 semitones.
- **Major Second**: 2 semitones.
- **Major Third**: 4 semitones.
- **Perfect Fifth**: 7 semitones.
- **Octave**: 12 semitones.
